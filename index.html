Questions You May Ask

Will my concrete floor scratch?

Concrete floors are highly durable, but like any surface, they can scratch under extreme conditions. However, with proper care and maintenance, including regular cleaning and occasional resealing, scratches can be minimized.

How long does it take to do a concrete floor?

The timeline for completing a concrete floor project depends on various factors, such as the size and complexity of the area, as well as the desired finish. Generally, it can take several days to complete, including preparation, pouring, curing, and finishing.

Is it easy to maintain a concrete floor?

Yes, concrete floors are relatively easy to maintain. Routine sweeping and mopping with a mild detergent are usually sufficient for day-to-day care. Additionally, periodic resealing helps to protect the surface and maintain its appearance over time.

How long after the concrete is poured can we use it?

While concrete can be walked on within hours of pouring, it’s best to wait at least 24 to 48 hours before placing heavy furniture or subjecting the surface to heavy foot traffic. Full curing typically takes about 28 days.
